About Me

A Clinician Who Truly Listens

I believe every voice deserves to be heard — and every child deserves the chance to find theirs.

I am a BASLP (Bachelor of Audiology & Speech-Language Pathology) trained clinician with extensive experience in speech and hearing rehabilitation. My practice is rooted in evidence-based therapy protocols, delivered with warmth and genuine care.

From diagnosing hearing loss in newborns to helping stroke survivors rediscover their words, I provide comprehensive audiological and speech-language services for patients across all age groups in Chandigarh and surrounding areas.
